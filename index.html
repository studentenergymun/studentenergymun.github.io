Be a Part of Student Energy at MUN

STUDENT CHAPTER AT MEMORIAL

"Empowering the next generation of energy leaders for a sustainable globe"

MUN chapter began in May 2022 to support and advance the academic, professional, and
social experience of students of Memorial University who care about a sustainable future.

Our Mission

The mission of the Student Energy chapter at the Memorial University of Newfoundland is to support sustainable energy initiatives in Atlantic Canada to address the need for providing clean, accessible, and affordable energy to different communities of the Atlantic region. we are inspired to foster sustainable energy transition by integrating academic research and education with our community's professional and social opportunities.

Our Goals

    ● To develop students' skills as problem-solvers while growing their skills beyond the traditional education to be able to address the diverse challenges the energy market faces.
    ● To raise awareness about the available opportunities to implement sustainable technologies for energy production in Atlantic Canada.
    ● To create an interdisciplinary hub for the students to keep pace with the technological development in the clean energy technologies.
    ● To provide opportunities for the students to get involved in the practical, sustainable projects.
